Exercise (from me): What is the average salary of employees who are still employed and working in departments 1 or 2, and whose birth dates fall between January 1, 1997 and December 31, 1998?
----------------------------
the solution : 
Select DepartmentID ,Averagee=avg(MonthlySalary) from employees 
where DepartmentID in(1,2)
and DateOfBirth BETWEEN '1997 -01-1' and '1998-12-31' 
and  ExitDate is null
GROUP BY DepartmentID;
