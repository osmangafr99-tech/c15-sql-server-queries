🧠 Exercise 1 – Simple inclusion

Find employees whose FirstName starts with “J”, “K”, or “L”.

🧠 Exercise 2 – Negation

Find employees whose FirstName does NOT start with “M” or “N”.

🧠 Exercise 3 – Range

Find employees whose FirstName starts with any letter from “D” to “H”.

🧠 Exercise 4 – Single character match

Find employees whose FirstName has “o” as the second character.

🧠 Exercise 5 – Combination

Find employees whose FirstName starts with “A” or “B” but the second character is NOT “l” or “r”.

Solution:
--Find employees whose FirstName starts with “J”, “K”, or “L”.
select ID,FirstName , LastName 
from Employees 
where FirstName like '[JKL]%';


--Find employees whose FirstName does NOT start with “M” or “N”.
select ID,FirstName , LastName 
from Employees 
where FirstName not like '[MN]%';


--Find employees whose FirstName starts with any letter from “D” to “H”.
select ID,FirstName , LastName 
from Employees 
where FirstName  like '[D-H]%'
order by FirstName;


--Find employees whose FirstName has “o” as the second character.
select ID,FirstName , LastName 
from Employees 
where FirstName  like '_o%'
order by FirstName;


--Find employees whose FirstName starts with “A” or “B” but the second character is NOT “l” or “r”.
select ID,FirstName , LastName 
from Employees 
where FirstName like 'A[^lr]%' or FirstName like 'B[^lr]%' ;
